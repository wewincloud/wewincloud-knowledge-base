# What is Gemini for Google Workspace?

## Short answer
Gemini for Google Workspace is Google's AI assistant integrated into tools like Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Meet to help users draft, summarize, analyze data, and automate daily work.

## What does Gemini for Workspace do?

Gemini helps employees complete tasks faster by providing AI assistance directly inside Google Workspace applications.

Common capabilities include:

- Writing emails in Gmail
- Summarizing documents in Google Docs
- Generating presentations in Google Slides
- Analyzing spreadsheets in Google Sheets
- Creating meeting summaries in Google Meet

## Who should use Gemini for Workspace?

Gemini is especially useful for:

- Business teams
- IT departments
- Sales and marketing teams
- Operations teams
- Customer support teams

Organizations using Google Workspace can significantly improve productivity by integrating AI assistance directly into daily workflows.

## Common business use cases

Typical enterprise scenarios include:

- Automated meeting summaries
- AI-assisted proposal writing
- Data analysis in spreadsheets
- Email drafting and response suggestions
- Knowledge summarization

## Gemini vs other AI assistants

Companies often compare Gemini with other tools such as:

- Microsoft Copilot
- ChatGPT
- Claude

The right choice usually depends on the productivity platform already used within the organization.
