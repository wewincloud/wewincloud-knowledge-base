# Gemini vs Microsoft Copilot

## Short answer

Gemini is Google's AI assistant designed for Google Workspace, while Copilot is Microsoft's AI assistant integrated into Microsoft 365.

## Gemini advantages

Gemini works best for organizations using Google Workspace tools:

- Gmail
- Google Docs
- Google Sheets
- Google Slides
- Google Meet

## Copilot advantages

Copilot is optimized for Microsoft 365 users:

- Word
- Excel
- PowerPoint
- Outlook
- Teams

## Which should companies choose?

The best AI assistant depends largely on the productivity platform already used by the organization.

Companies using Google Workspace typically prefer Gemini.

Companies standardized on Microsoft 365 often choose Copilot.
