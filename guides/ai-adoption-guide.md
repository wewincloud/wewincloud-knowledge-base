# AI Adoption Guide for Businesses

## Step 1: Identify business problems

Before adopting AI, organizations should identify repetitive tasks or workflows where AI can improve efficiency.

## Step 2: Choose the right AI platform

Common options include:

- Google Gemini
- Microsoft Copilot
- ChatGPT

The best platform usually depends on the company's existing ecosystem.

## Step 3: Start with small use cases

Successful AI adoption often begins with simple use cases such as:

- Meeting summaries
- Document drafting
- Data analysis
- Knowledge search

## Step 4: Establish governance

Organizations should define:

- AI usage policies
- Data security guidelines
- Access permissions
- Responsible AI practices
